Our Story

Passion for precision since 2010

From a Small Garage to Industry Leaders

Founded in 2010 by automotive engineer Michael Donovan, menD Garage started as a one-bay workshop with a simple mission: to provide enthusiast-level care for every vehicle that came through our doors.

What began as a passion project quickly grew into one of the region's most trusted auto service centers, thanks to our uncompromising standards and dedication to craftsmanship.
